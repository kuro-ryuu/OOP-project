Scenario: Restaurant

- Variability:
+ Accidents inside the kitchen.
+ Equipment malfunction.
+ Supply quality drops.
+ …

- Interconnectedness:
+ Restaurant order supply from third parties -> Supply gets delivered in bulk 
-> Supplies get used to make food by chefs and cooks -> Food then gets served by waiters/waitresses

- Complexity:
+ Waitresses/Waiters are not guaranteed to take the shortest path every time they pick up the food and go to the table.
+ The served portions are not guaranteed to be the exact same every time.
+ Service’s quality may fluctuate through time depending on the batch of staff.
